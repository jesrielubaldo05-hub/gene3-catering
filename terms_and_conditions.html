Terms and Condition

Reservation Fee

  • A minimum of PHP 6,000.00 non-refundable reservation fee is required to secure your booking.
  • This fee is deductible from the total contract package. We do not pencil book any event.

Down payment

  • A 30% non-refundable down payment must be settled at least 6 months before the event date.

Remaining Balance

  • The remaining 10% balance shall be paid on or before the event day.

Postponement

  • A rebooking fee of 10% of the total package cost will be charged for any rescheduled event made no later than 1 month before the original booking date. Rebooking is subject to service availability.
  • The rescheduled event date must fall within the same month as the original booking.

Cancellation

  • In case of cancellation, the reservation fee and 30% of the contract price are non-refundable.

Overtime Charge

  • Catering service is strictly for 4 hours. Any additional hours will be charged PHP 150.00 per staff per hour.

Floor Charge

  • A floor charge of PHP 1,000 per floor applies for events held on the second floor or higher floors without a service elevator, or if the venue is more than 40 meters from the drop-off point. If the venue stairs are steeper or higher than initially discussed, a regular floor charge of PHP 1,500.00 will apply.

Gate/Parking Fees

  • Clients will shoulder any entrance, gate, or parking fees.

Mutual Understanding

  • Clients must provide meals and drinks for crew members after venue setup. All parties, including clients and guest, are expected to maintain politeness and respect during the event.

Lechon Chopping

  • A PHP 1,000.00 fee applies if waiters are asked to chop lechon. Please notify us in advance so we can bring the necessary tools (knife, chopping board, etc.).

Outside Food

  • If clients choose to bring their own food, Gene 3's Catering is not liable for any health risks (e.g., food poisoning, diarrhea) that may arise. This includes risks from air-transmitted bacteria or improperly stored food.

Setup Time

  • Gene 3's Catering requires a setup time of 3-4 hours. We are not liable for delays caused by uncooperative venue management or inconsistencies in on-the-day arrangements.

Agreement

  • All clients are required to read, understand, and agree to the terms of this contract before making any payments. Ignorance of these terms will result in penalties at the discretion of Gene 3's Catering management.

Security Deposit

  • A refundable security bond of PHP 3,000.00 is required to cover potential damages or losses to catering utensils, silverware, chinaware, and other equipment. This deposit will be refunded within a week after the event, subject to deductions for damages, if any.

Transportation Charge

  • A transportation charge applies based on the venue location.

Crew Meals

  • If crew members are not permitted to eat at the buffet area, the client must provide decent meals. If crew members are allowed to eat at the buffet, additional crew meals will be included in the final bill.

Note: Any additional charges apply if crew members cannot eat at the buffet.
