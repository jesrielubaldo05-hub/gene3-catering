Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Gene 3's Catering Service ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone / contact number
  • Home or event address
  • Event details (type, date, venue, number of guests)
  • Payment information (down payment amount and payment method)
  • Messages or feedback you submit through our forms
  • Photos or videos you upload with your reviews

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your booking or reservation inquiries
  • Contact you to confirm or follow up on your event
  • Respond to your messages and feedback
  • Improve our catering services based on your reviews
  • Send you updates, promotions, or service-related communications (you may unsubscribe at any time)

3. How We Store Your Information

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data remains within our team and is only used for purposes directly related to your event or inquiry with Gene 3's Catering Service.

5. Cookies

Our website may use basic browser features to improve your experience. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for promotional communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at gene3s@gmail.com or call 091-2314-2562.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
